What it does

A simple vault for everyday documents

Category based storage

Separate documents into General, Study, Personal details, Finance, Work, Medical, and Legal sections.

Fast upload and download

Add PDFs, images, Word files, Excel sheets, text files, and CSV files, then download them anytime.

Search and filters

Find files by name, description, tags, file type, or document category without digging through folders.

Private user accounts

Each user sees only their own files. Authentication keeps the dashboard and document APIs protected.

How to use

From upload to download in four steps

  1. Create an account or sign in. Use your username and password to open your private dashboard.
  2. Upload your file. Drop files into the upload area or click the upload box.
  3. Add details. Choose a category, add an optional description, and add tags for better searching.
  4. Manage your vault. Search, filter, download, or delete documents whenever needed.

Best for storing

  • Study notes, assignments, certificates
  • Personal identity and address documents
  • Tax, bank, invoice, and finance files
  • Work documents and project records
  • Medical and legal paperwork
